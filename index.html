Metzola is a deep-tech startup developing the V1 — an autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV). Leveraging Blue-LED optical communication and Edge AI, we enable high-speed data transfer and real-time navigation beneath the surface.

Core Technology

Blue-LED Communication

High-speed underwater data transmission using optical signals, overcoming traditional acoustic limitations.

Edge AI Navigation

Real-time decision making and autonomous movement without reliance on constant remote control.

Long Duration Operation

Designed for extended missions, enabling persistent monitoring and data collection in challenging environments.

Research

Oceanographic data collection, environmental monitoring, and scientific exploration.

Infrastructure

Inspection and maintenance of underwater structures, pipelines, and vessels.

Defense

Advanced autonomous capabilities for security, surveillance, and strategic operations.
